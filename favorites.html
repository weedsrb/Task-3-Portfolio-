My Favorite Things

A glimpse into my personal interests and passions

Cuisine

Middle Eastern Cuisine

Growing up in Palestine, I developed a deep appreciation for our rich culinary traditions. I love cooking traditional dishes like Maqluba (upside-down rice and vegetables), Musakhan (sumac chicken with onions), and Knafeh (sweet cheese pastry).

Cooking these dishes connects me to my heritage and brings a taste of home to Budapest. I often host dinner parties for friends, introducing them to Palestinian cuisine and culture.

Sport

Football

Football has been my passion since childhood. I played for my school team in Palestine and continue to play recreationally in Budapest. I'm a dedicated fan of FC Barcelona and follow their matches religiously.

Beyond watching and playing, I'm fascinated by the tactical aspects of the game. I enjoy analyzing different playing styles and strategies. Football has taught me valuable lessons about teamwork, discipline, and perseverance.

Hobby

Photography

Photography allows me to capture moments and tell stories visually. I'm particularly drawn to street photography and landscapes. Budapest, with its stunning architecture and vibrant street life, provides endless inspiration.

I've been developing this hobby for the past three years, learning about composition, lighting, and post-processing. Photography has enhanced my attention to detail and visual thinking, skills that also benefit my work in computer science.
