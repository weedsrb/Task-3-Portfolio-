My Story

I'm Waleed Barghouthi, a Palestinian Computer Science student in my final year at Eötvös Loránd University (ELTE) in Budapest, Hungary. My journey in technology began at a young age when I first discovered my passion for computers and problem-solving.

Growing up in Palestine, I faced unique challenges that shaped my perspective and fueled my determination to excel in the field of computer science. The limited technological resources in my hometown inspired me to find creative solutions and develop a deep appreciation for the transformative power of technology.

In 2021, I was awarded a scholarship to study at ELTE University in Budapest, which opened up new opportunities for me to pursue my academic and professional goals in a supportive and innovative environment.
